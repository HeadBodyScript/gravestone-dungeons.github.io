What is Gravestone Dungeons



In a world where swords clash, shadows rise, and ancient secrets lie buried...
An epic adventure awaits.
From the darkest depths of forgotten tombs to the icy peaks of untamed dimensions, this datapack breathes life into the medieval Minecraft experience. A realm reborn with challenge, mystery, and untold power.
Dungeons now tower and sprawl across every biome and realm. Inspired by Dungeon Crawl and When Dungeons Arise, each stronghold teems with traps, treasure, and terror. Face off against custom bosses, conquer cursed halls, and unearth loot unlike anything seen in vanilla. Enchanted blades, arcane relics, and uncraftable tools that shimmer with elemental fury.
Each weapon is more than steel. It’s a legend forged. With secondary abilities powered by experience, time, or shadow, wielders unleash particle storms, potion effects, and devastating techniques. But power comes at a cost… some blades hunger for your XP.
At the heart of it all lies Direwood Keep... a fortress bustling with traders, wanderers, and secrets. Here, gold and silver coins are currency, and merchants like Cloud Nomads and Rogue Traders deal in exotic wares. Among them is Monk Zaccarius, guardian of the ancient tomes, guiding heroes on how to reinforce weapons through forgotten arts. And Eldrid, the mapkeeper, who holds the keys to every dungeon’s doorstep.
Magic, mystery, and mercenaries.
Glory, gold, and grim battles.
This is no ordinary datapack.
This is your medieval saga.

Bosses

Mechanics

Boss fights activate when a player enters the boss room. When the boss fight gets triggered any players in the dungeon will be teleported into the dungeon's boss room
The boss fight will end when all players leave the area, get killed or when the boss dies
Boss fights will not get triggered in peaceful mode
Boss stats scale with difficulty and active players in the boss room

Enemies

Every dungeon has unique enemies to fight, Each spawned through trail spawners that give loot at the end. These enemies are empowered when you enter the dungeon while you have the Ominous effect active
An empowered dungeon will significantly increase the difficulty of the enemies and increase the rewards
BEWARE. Enemies may spawn with new enchantments

Runes

    • Rune of Greater Health
    • Grants the player +2 Max Health when consumed (Max = 20 Max Health)
    • Aquired from Alaric Stormweaver for 2 Gold Coins and boss loot

    • Rune of Magicka
    • Grants the player +1000 Max Magicka when consumed (Max = 16000)
    • Aquired from Alaric Stormweaver for 2 Gold Coins and boss loot

    • Rune of Lesser Fall Damage
    • Grants the player +1 block Safe Fall Distance when consumed (Max = 10)
    • Aquired from Alaric Stormweaver for 2 Gold Coins and boss loot

    • Rune of Higher Ranger
    • Grants the player +1 Interaction Range when consumed (Max = 10)
    • Aquired from Alaric Stormweaver for 2 Gold Coins and boss loot

    • Rune of Longer Breath
    • Grants the player +20% Longer Breath when consumed (Max = 200%)
    • Aquired from Alaric Stormweaver for 2 Gold Coins and boss loot

    • Rune of Concinnity
    • Resets all runes for the player when consumed
    • Aquired from Alaric Stormweaver for 2 Gold Coins and boss loot

Reinforced Items

Items that are reinforced have additional durability
Very low durability: 0.5x
Low durability: 1x
Moderate durability: 2x
High durability: 4x
Very High Durability: 8x

Currency

Within the realm, you may discover treasures.. one of which are coins, such as Gold Coins and Silver Coins. These coins can be used at any of the new villagers found throughout the lands
There are more ways to obtain coins, such as slaying bosses, trading with villagers, or using the enchantment 'Bounty Claim'
